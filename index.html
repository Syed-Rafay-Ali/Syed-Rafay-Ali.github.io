About Me

I am a dedicated and ambitious Master of Data Science student at Macquarie University, Sydney, with a strong academic foundation in Computer Science from Forman Christian College University, Lahore. My academic journey has equipped me with a robust skill set in programming languages such as Python, PHP, JavaScript, C, and C++, and extensive experience with tools like Anaconda, Google Colab, SQL, and MYSQL. My passion for data engineering is particularly focused on the fascinating field of Natural Language Processing (NLP).

During my academic career, I have undertaken various projects that blend my interests in data science and engineering with practical applications. For instance, my Smart Invigilator project utilized Python, OpenCV, and Tkinter to develop a desktop application that employs facial and voice recognition to monitor student attention during exams. This project highlights my ability to integrate different technologies to solve real-world problems effectively.

I am now looking forward to leveraging my academic knowledge and practical experience in data science and natural language processing to drive impactful projects and advancements in the field.
